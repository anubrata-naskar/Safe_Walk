At Safe Walk, our mission is simple: to help women feel safer and more confident while moving through public spaces. We believe safety is a right, not a privilege — and we’re building technology that supports that vision. Our platform empowers users by showing real-time safety parameters for their current location — including lighting, foot traffic, crime reports, and recent user reviews. With just a glance, you can see how safe your surroundings are and make informed decisions about your route.

Even more importantly, Safe Walk highlights nearby help zones, such as police stations, hospitals, open businesses, safe spaces, and community hubs — so you always know where to turn if you need assistance. Whether you’re walking home late, navigating a new area, or just checking in before heading out, Safe Walk is your personal safety companion. Together, we’re creating a world where women walk free, walk strong — and walk safe.
